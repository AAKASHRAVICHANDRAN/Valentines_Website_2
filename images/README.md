# Images Folder 📸

This folder is where you can add your personal photos to make the birthday and Valentine's Day page even more special!

## How to Add Your Photos

1. **Choose your favorite photos** - Select up to 4 beautiful memories you want to display
2. **Rename your photos** to match these exact names:
   - `photo1.jpg` - First photo slot
   - `photo2.jpg` - Second photo slot  
   - `photo3.jpg` - Third photo slot
   - `photo4.jpg` - Fourth photo slot

3. **Copy the renamed photos** into this `images` folder
4. **Refresh the webpage** to see your photos appear!

## Supported Image Formats
- `.jpg` or `.jpeg` (recommended)
- `.png` 
- `.webp`

## Tips for Best Results
- **Square photos work best** - they'll fit perfectly in the gallery grid
- **High resolution** photos will look crisp and beautiful
- **Good lighting** makes photos pop on the romantic background
- **Mix of photos** - consider including:
  - Wedding photos
  - Vacation memories
  - Candid moments
  - Special occasions

## Photo Ideas 💕
- Your wedding day
- Anniversary celebrations
- Romantic getaways
- Everyday moments that make you smile
- Holiday memories
- Date nights
- Adventures together

## Troubleshooting
- If photos don't appear, check that:
  - File names match exactly (photo1.jpg, photo2.jpg, etc.)
  - Files are in this `images` folder
  - You've refreshed the webpage
  - Image files aren't corrupted

## Interactive Features
Once your photos are added:
- **Click on any photo** to view it in full size
- **Click anywhere on the page** for floating hearts
- **Use keyboard shortcuts**: H for hearts, S for sparkles, C for celebration
- **Click the surprise button** for a special message

Enjoy creating beautiful memories! 💖
